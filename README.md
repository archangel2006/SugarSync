# SugarSync

SugarSync is a simple web-based health companion designed to help users manage diabetes-related wellness in one place. The project combines onboarding, glucose tracking, medication reminders, health reports, nutrition guidance, fitness support, and community engagement into an accessible mobile-friendly experience.

## Problem
Many people managing diabetes need a centralized place to monitor their health habits, track blood sugar trends, follow medication schedules, and stay motivated. Without a simple tool, important health information can become scattered across different apps, notes, or paper records.

## Solution
SugarSync provides a unified dashboard-style experience where users can:
- sign up and access a personalized health journey,
- track glucose levels and view visual summaries,
- review monthly health reports,
- manage medications and reminders,
- explore nutrition and fitness support,
- connect with community groups for encouragement and support.

## Key Features
- User registration and welcome flow
- Glucose monitoring with visual charts and meter-style indicators
- Health report summaries for key wellness metrics
- Medication planning with calendar-based organization
- Nutrition guidance section for smarter meal choices
- Fitness activity support for healthy routines
- Community support pages with searchable communities
- Clean, responsive web interface with simple navigation

## How It Works
1. Open the landing page from [index.html](index.html).
2. Create an account or continue through the home screen.
3. Navigate to the different health modules for glucose tracking, reports, medications, nutrition, fitness, and community support.
